\subsection{Area  and Volumes}
Process for finding areas and volumes 
\begin{enumerate}
	\item Draw a picture
	\item Identify limits of Integration 
	\item Identify integrand
	\item integrate to solve
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Volume by Slicing}
   \[
	dv = A(x) \; dx
   \]
   $dx$ : Width of slice , an $A(x)$ is cross sectional area
   then volume of entire loaf is given by , 
      \[
   	v = \int A(x) \; dx
      \]
